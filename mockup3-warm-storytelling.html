The Story

A Little About the Music

For years, Larry has been writing and recording music purely for the joy of it — songs about family, love, life, and everything in between.

From heartfelt tributes to the people he loves most, to feel-good tunes that make you tap your foot and smile, every song here comes from a genuine place. No record labels, no algorithms — just real songs from a real person.

This is the home for all of it. Pull up a chair, press play, and stay a while.

"Music is what happens when you stop thinking and start feeling."
