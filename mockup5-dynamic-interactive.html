About Larry

Larry Lunt makes music for the pure joy of it. No record labels, no touring schedules, no industry machinery -- just a guy with a guitar, a melody in his head, and people he loves to write songs for.

Over the years, Larry has built a catalog of original songs that span heartfelt ballads, playful sing-alongs, and everything in between. Each track is a snapshot of a moment, a feeling, or a person close to his heart -- from thank-you letters set to music for his grandmother, to upbeat tunes that just make people smile.

"Music doesn't have to be a career to be meaningful. Sometimes the best songs are the ones written at the kitchen table for the people sitting around it."
